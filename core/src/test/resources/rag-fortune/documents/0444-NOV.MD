**Company Profile: NOV**

Ranked 444th in the Fortune 2024 rankings, NOV is a prominent player in the energy sector, specifically in the oil and gas equipment and services industry. As a profitable company, NOV has demonstrated its ability to navigate the complex and often volatile energy market.

Founded by a team of entrepreneurs, NOV is not led by its founder, but rather by its CEO, Clay C. Williams. Under Williams' leadership, the company has experienced significant growth, with a notable increase in jobs, indicating a positive trend in its workforce expansion.

In the 2024 Fortune rankings, NOV gained 57 places, moving up to 444th, a testament to its improving performance. This growth has also earned NOV the distinction of being a newcomer to the Fortune 500 list, a prestigious recognition of the company's accomplishments.

As of March 28, 2024, NOV's market value stood at approximately $7.69 billion, with revenues of $8.58 billion, representing an 18.6% increase from the previous year. The company's profits have also shown impressive growth, with a remarkable 540.6% increase, amounting to $993 million. NOV's assets were valued at $11.29 billion, further solidifying its position in the industry.

Headquartered in Houston, Texas, NOV employs a workforce of 33,676 individuals, contributing to the local economy and demonstrating its commitment to job creation. As a public company, NOV is listed on the stock exchange under the ticker symbol NOV.

Despite not being recognized as one of the World's Most Admired Companies or one of the Best Companies to Work For, NOV's growth and financial performance suggest a strong foundation for future success. The company's website, www.nov.com, provides further information about its operations, products, and services.

NOV's presence in the Fortune 2024 rankings highlights its emergence as a major player in the energy sector, with a strong potential for continued growth and expansion. As the energy landscape continues to evolve, NOV is well-positioned to capitalize on new opportunities and solidify its position in the industry.
